Aka & Geruma · Overview

Aka & Geruma: a quiet Kerama guide阿嘉・慶留間 — 静かな慶良間ガイド

Okinawa Island Guide · 2026Okinawa Island Guide · 2026

If Zamami is the lively heart of the Keramas, Aka and Geruma are its peaceful neighbours — the same astonishing water, a slower pace, and a famous resident: the wild Kerama deer. The two islands are joined by a bridge, so you can wander between them on foot or by bike. 座間味が慶良間の賑やかな中心なら、阿嘉と慶留間は静かなお隣。同じ驚きの海を、よりゆったりと。名物は野生のケラマジカ。2島は橋でつながり、徒歩や自転車で行き来できます。

Nishibama Beachニシバマビーチ

Aka's signature beach is a long, gentle curve of white sand and clear water on the island's north-east side — superb for snorkeling and famously photogenic. Gear rentals and a beach hut operate in season. 阿嘉を代表するビーチは、島の北東に伸びる白砂のゆるやかな弧と澄んだ海。シュノーケルに最適で、写真映えも抜群。シーズン中は用具レンタルや浜の小屋があります。

Kerama deerケラマジカ

The Kerama deer — a small, protected native deer designated a national natural monument — roam Aka and Geruma. You may spot them near the village or along quiet roads, especially around dawn and dusk. Watch from a distance and never feed them. 国の天然記念物に指定された在来の小型シカ「ケラマジカ」が阿嘉・慶留間に生息。集落付近や静かな道沿いで、特に朝夕に見かけることがあります。距離を保ち、餌は与えないこと。

Aka, Geruma & the bridge阿嘉・慶留間と橋

Geruma is linked to Aka by the Aka Ohashi bridge, and the small Kerama Airport sits on the neighbouring islet of Fukaji. It's a tiny, quiet world that's easy to explore slowly. 慶留間は阿嘉大橋で阿嘉とつながり、隣の外地島には小さな慶良間空港があります。こぢんまりと静かで、ゆっくり巡るのに向いた世界です。

Getting there行き方

Boats from Tomari Port in Naha serve Aka (the high-speed Queen Zamami calls at Aka; some sailings link Aka and Zamami). See our Kerama access guide, and check the daily ferry forecast before you go. 那覇・泊港からの船が阿嘉を結びます（高速船クイーンざまみが阿嘉に寄港。阿嘉〜座間味を結ぶ便も）。慶良間アクセスガイドと、出発前に毎日の欠航予報を確認しましょう。
