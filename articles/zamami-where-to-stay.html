Zamami is small, so almost everywhere is close to the port and beaches — what really differs is the style of stay. Tell us what matters most and filter the list below. Most places are family-run guesthouses (minshuku); book early for summer and the whale season. 座間味は小さいので、宿はどこも港やビーチに近め。違うのは泊まる「スタイル」です。重視したいもので下のリストを絞り込んでください。多くは家族経営の民宿。夏とクジラの季節は早めの予約を。
